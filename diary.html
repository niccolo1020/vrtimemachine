My Day

I live in Europe and weigh 22 kilograms.

This morning I wore my uniform with a St. George's logo on it. I put my Nike sneakers into my PE Kit. Then my mom used a thermos to keep her coffee hot.

In school we learn about music, philosophy, geography, and astronomy. We exercise in the school gym.

On the school bus sometimes there is anarchy. But today, we all read a book on dinosaurs.

Sometimes when I realize I have not finished my homework, I panic

After I finish my homework, I play Titanfall on the XBox.

Next Saturday there will be the Rome Marathon.

By Niccolò Marino
